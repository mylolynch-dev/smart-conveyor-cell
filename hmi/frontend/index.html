Protocol Notes — Where Real Plant Protocols Would Fit
Modbus TCP (implemented here) — Used for simple PLC-to-SCADA polling. No device discovery, no implicit messaging. Good for legacy equipment and simple IO. Widely supported.

EtherNet/IP — Allen-Bradley standard. Uses CIP (Common Industrial Protocol). Supports implicit (cyclic I/O) and explicit (request/response) messaging. Required for Rockwell PLCs in real plants.

PROFINET — Siemens standard. Real-time Ethernet for motion and safety. Two timing classes: RT (10ms) and IRT (1ms). Required for Siemens S7 PLCs.

IO-Link — Point-to-point sensor/actuator bus. Sits below fieldbus layer. Used for smart sensors with parameterization and diagnostics.

DeviceNet — CAN-based fieldbus. Common in older Rockwell installations. Being superseded by EtherNet/IP.
